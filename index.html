Elizabeth Taylor's Jewellery Collection at Bulgari

Discover the gems entangled in Hollywood's most infamous love story at the jeweller's London store

Save for later

Richard Burton once said, “The only word Elizabeth Taylor knows in Italian is Bulgari.” The jeweller’s lavish gems became a lifelong source of happiness for the actress, whose collection is the most magnificent in the world. Now, from 27 June to 17 July, her collection will be on display at the newly opened New Bond Street Bulgari store.

In 2011, Bulgari reacquired seven of the pieces that will be on display at a Christie’s sale. It was the most valuable sale of jewellery in auction history, making a total of £100,324,209.

The collection is reflective of the actress’s love of Bulgari jewels, but also a testament to her relationship with Burton. The exhibition includes the ring that marked their engagement in 1962 – a gigantic octagonal step-cut Colombian emerald green rock, wrapped in pear shaped diamonds, with a platinum band – a matching brooch (later made into a pendant); and a necklace bearing 16 of the same Colombian emeralds, each surrounded by its own cluster of diamonds. This was the necklace that Burton presented to Taylor at their wedding, to which the aforementioned pendant was an attachable centrepiece.

(Taylor wearing the diamond and emerald Bulgari necklace given to her by Richard Burton)

Of Burton’s gifts, Taylor said, “I used to get so excited I would jump on top of him and practically make love to him in Bulgari.”

Legend has it that it was love at first sight for the co-stars Taylor and Burton on their first day shooting Cleopatra in 1962. Both still married, the adulterous relationship that ensued became known as Le Scandale; paparazzi arrived en masse and a wildfire of press attacked. Forget romantic evenings at the Trevi Fountain - a secret door at the back of the Bulgari shop on Via dei Condotti was the couple’s refuge. In the enclosed back room, amongst dazzling jewels, they were safe.

Elizabeth Taylor’s Bulgari collection tells a story of lavish glamour, encapsulated by a fiery romance of Hollywood’s greatest proportions.
